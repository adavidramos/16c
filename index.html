Run with us

We're a friendly DC running group at the corner of Adams Morgan, Columbia Heights, and Mount Pleasant.

We meet Mondays/Wednesdays, 6:30 am at Rabaut Park, 16th and Columbia NW. (We're typically running 4–4.5 miles at 8:00–10:00 min/mi, though we welcome runners at any pace.)

Track workouts Thursdays, 6:30 am at Banneker Recreation Center track, Barry Place and 9th St. NW. (Cardozo track has been closed lately, so we are at Banneker Rec for the summer.)

Please join! If you're new, please sign our waiver and feel free to give us an email with any questions. We update Twitter and Facebook if we have to cancel or reschedule runs.
